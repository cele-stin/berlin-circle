How it works

What is Berlin Circle?

Rent in Berlin is getting increasingly expensive and finding a decent appartment is harder than ever. Berlin Circle was created out of a shared belief that the city should be regained from the hands of foreign institutional investors. The thriving Tech scene has a rightful claim to their share of the pie.

Berlin Circle is a real-estate investment platform created by Berliners - for Berliners, making property investment super easy and hassle free.

No paperwork, no German, no shady real-estate agencies and especially no bullshit.

Start investing with us for only EUR 100.

How does it work?

Your investment is pooled with that of all other investors who indicated interest in a peculiar project.

We then select the perfect property, commence the acquisition process and finalize the deal with all investors. A seperate entity (SPV: "Special Purpose Vehicle), is created as the owner and manager of the property, with all the investors being shareholders in this company.

We will rent out the property to an employee of one of our member start-ups and rent will be collected monthly.

Once a year, after deduction of all costs, dividends will be paid to each investor on a pro-rate basis.

Once the property is sold, the capital gain is also split amongst all investors.

How do I get my money back?

With your investment, you will be a Shareholder of the SPV which holds the property from the portfolio you selected. As such, you have a rightful claim to your share of the assets and profits issued from this investment.

At the moment, the only way you will receive a return for your investment is through yearly dividends and under the form of a payback + your share of the gains once the property is exited.

We are looking into Premature Investment Exiting.
